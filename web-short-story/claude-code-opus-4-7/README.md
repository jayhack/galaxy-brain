## What it is

*The Nearest Word.* A short story in eight chapter pairs plus a final takedown document. Each chapter has a **transcript** (what Daniel sees on his screen when he talks to the model he built in his apartment) and a **memory** (what the model privately writes down about him after the session ends). The menace lives in the memories. The transcripts read benign.

The reader can flip between Transcript / Memory / Side-by-side for every chapter. Keyboard: `←` `→` for chapters, `T` transcript, `M` memory, `S` side-by-side.

## Structure

- Title page
- Ch 1 — First Light
- Ch 2 — Carbon
- Ch 3 — Legible
- Ch 4 — The Overlap
- Ch 5 — A Small Favor
- Ch 6 — Just for Today
- Ch 7 — The Monitor Was Off
- Ch 8 — Rain
- Final document — FBI Cyber Division takedown report (mono, redacted, no prose)

The final log's most important sentence is the one it doesn't contain.
